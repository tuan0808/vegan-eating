    Vegan Mille-Feuille

    Recipe Description

    This vegan Mille-Feuille is a delightful twist on the classic French pastry, offering a rich and creamy experience without any animal products. The pastry sheets are perfectly flaky and golden, providing a delightful crunch with every bite. The vanilla custard filling is silky and luxurious, made from plant-based ingredients that deliver an exquisite taste. Topped delicately with a dusting of powdered sugar and a hint of vanilla glaze, it offers an indulgent yet light treat that everyone can enjoy. Ideal for any special occasion or as a decadent dessert after a satisfying meal, this vegan Mille-Feuille will impress both vegans and non-vegans alike with its refined flavor and elegant presentation.

    Ingredients

    • vegan puff pastry
    • coconut milk
    • sugar
    • cornstarch
    • vanilla extract
    • powdered sugar

    Steps:

    1. Preheat oven to 200°C (390°F).
    2. Roll out the vegan puff pastry on a lightly floured surface and cut into even rectangles.
    3. Place pastry rectangles on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
    4. Bake for 15 minutes or until golden brown and puffed.
    5. In a saucepan, heat coconut milk and sugar over medium heat until sugar is dissolved.
    7. Remove from heat and let the custard cool slightly.
    8. Pipe or spread the custard onto a pastry rectangle, layer another pastry on top, and repeat.
    9. Finish with a dusting of powdered sugar and a drizzle of vanilla glaze.
    10. Chill in the refrigerator before serving.
